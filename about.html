Who We Are

Ananta is named after the Sanskrit word, "an-ant", meaning endless or eternal. Our vision is driven by the belief that there is no end to learning as it's present in everything.

Ananta is home to a unique environment that offers overall development and life-long learning to young children, starting from the tender age of 1.5 years.

14000 Sqft campus at Khajaguda

Our Beautiful Campus

At Ananta, we strongly believe that learning is a lifelong experience which begins at birth. That's why, we offer Montessori Education to young children from the tender age of 1.5 years so that they grow in every aspect of life.

We focus on emotional, social, cognitive, physical and mental development of children, thus providing a nurturing environment for their holistic growth.
